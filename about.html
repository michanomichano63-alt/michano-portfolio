My Story

My name is Jackson Workanuan, born in Nimba County and raised in Monrovia, Liberia. Growing up, music became a major part of my identity. I love listening to music every single day because it inspires me, motivates me, and gives me a way to speak to the world.

I graduated from YMCA High School in 2021 and started studying Information Technology at Starz University in 2024. I chose IT because in Liberia, music alone is not a stable or lucrative career — but my passion for music has never stopped.

As an artist, I mostly sing about real-life situations. My music carries messages that reflect struggles, dreams, relationships, and street experiences. When I sing, I deliver truth — not fiction.

Today, I am building my name Michano into a strong Liberian brand, one song at a time.
